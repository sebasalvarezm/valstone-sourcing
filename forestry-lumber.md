# Forestry & Lumber

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Timberland owners, logging companies and contractors, sawmills and lumber mills, pulp and paper producers.

**Workflows:** Harvest planning, logging operations, land management, environmental monitoring, compliance reporting.

**Types of Solutions:** Forestry management and GIS platforms, timber harvest and supply chain software, sustainability and compliance tools.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Forest Products and Lumber group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs sawmills and wood products operations, across log and lumber tracking, production planning, yield and grading, inventory, shipping, billing, and customer experience. In simple terms, we help operators turn raw material into finished product, and get it to customers reliably, with fewer manual steps.
