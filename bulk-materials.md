# Bulk Materials (Construction Materials & Building Products)

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Cement and concrete producers, asphalt plants, aggregate suppliers, waste firms, building material distributors.

**Workflows:** Production, quoting, billing, order management, weighing and scale ticketing, dispatching, quality and compliance.

**Types of Solutions:** Batch control systems, scale and ticketing and inventory management systems, transportation management systems.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Construction Materials and Building Products group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs aggregates, concrete, asphalt, and building products operations, across quoting, order-to-cash, scale and load workflows, dispatch and delivery, inventory and production, quality and compliance, billing, and customer experience. In simple terms, we help operators move material from plant or pit to jobsite with less friction and fewer manual steps.
