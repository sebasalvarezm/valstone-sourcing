# Mining

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Mine operators, mineral exploration firms, quarries and pit owners, drilling and blasting contractors.

**Workflows:** Exploration and mine planning, drilling and extraction operations, equipment maintenance and management, compliance reporting.

**Types of Solutions:** Geological modeling and mine design software, fleet and equipment management systems, mining ERP, EHS platforms.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Mining and Minerals group. The vision is to become a long-term home for software that supports mine and quarry operations, across planning and production reporting, fleet and maintenance workflows, dispatch and hauling, inventory and stockpiles, processing and quality, compliance, billing, and customer experience. In simple terms, we help operators move ore and materials from pit to plant to customer, with fewer manual steps and better visibility.
