# Waste, Recycling & Environmental Services

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Municipalities, private waste haulers, recycling centers, landfill and transfer stations, environmental services firms.

**Workflows:** Route planning and dispatch, collection and disposal management, recycling and compliance reporting, landfill operations.

**Types of Solutions:** Waste ERP and billing systems, route optimization and fleet tracking software, recycling management software.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Waste, Recycling, and Environmental Services group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs waste collection, transfer, recycling, and landfill operations, across customer setup and pricing, route planning and dispatch, in-cab mobile workflows, scale house and weighbridge transactions, ticketing and billing, material and load tracking, compliance and regulatory reporting, and customer experience. In simple terms, we help operators move waste from curb or gate to final disposition with less friction and fewer manual steps.
