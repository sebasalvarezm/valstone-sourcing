# Contractor Solutions (Contractor Operations & Project Delivery)

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** General contractors, specialty subcontractors, construction project managers.

**Workflows:** Cost estimating, bidding, project scheduling, project management and controls, inspections, field reporting.

**Types of Solutions:** Construction management software, estimating and bidding tools, field management and compliance tools.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Contractor Operations and Project Delivery group. The vision is to become a long-term home for software that helps contractors win work and deliver projects smoothly, across estimating and bids, project controls, field reporting, inspections, closeout, subcontractor compliance, billing, and customer experience. In simple terms, we want to help teams finish jobs faster, with fewer surprises and less paperwork.
