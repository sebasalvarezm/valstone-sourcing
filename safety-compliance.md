# Safety & Compliance (Control of Work)

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Manufacturing and engineering and construction firms, energy and mineral producers and distributors.

**Workflows:** Safety training and certification, compliance audits and inspections, incident tracking and reporting, permit management.

**Types of Solutions:** Safety management and training systems, audit and incident reporting tools, permit management systems.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Safety, Compliance, and Control-of-Work group. The vision is to become a long-term home for software that helps industrial operators run safer sites and keep assets reliable, across permits-to-work, lockout tagout and isolations, inspections, incident reporting, maintenance, audits, training, contractor management, regulatory compliance, and maintenance execution such as work orders, preventive maintenance, and mobile field checklists. In simple terms, we help teams make sure the right work happens the right way, every day.
