# Bulk Liquids & Fuels

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Oil and fuel distributors, chemical producers and distributors, bulk liquid terminal operators and transporters.

**Workflows:** Tank monitoring and inventory control, loading and unloading, delivery scheduling and routing, billing and customer experience.

**Types of Solutions:** Terminal management and monitoring systems, fleet routing software, customer billing and invoicing tools.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Bulk Liquids and Fuels group. Our vision is to become a long-term home for software that runs fuel and bulk liquid distribution, across terminals and bulk storage, pricing, dispatch and delivery, inventory and reconciliation, compliance, billing, and customer experience. The end goal is to help operators move product safely and on time, with less friction and fewer manual steps.
